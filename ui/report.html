Budalangi Floods

by ed

Location: Budalangi

Type of Report: Intervention Report

I am a resident of budalangi. I, along with many of my neighbours, have lost our homes due to floods.

We are requesting that the government provide the following.

  • Flood draining solutions.
  • Temporary housing.
  • Temporary schools for our children.
  • Hospital services for residents who have gotten water-borne diseases.
